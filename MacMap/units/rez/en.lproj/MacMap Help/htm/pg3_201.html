XMap - Join Options

The join options let you set the distance limits for the projection of a point entered on an existing object. There are 4 types of joins:
At Shortest: the entry point is projected on the segmentation of the support object at the closest coordinate.
In the Direction: the entry point is projected on the segmentation of the support object at the coordinate obtained from the intersection between the right-hand side defined by the last 2 points entered and the closest segment of the support object intersecting that right-hand side.
At Angularities: the entry point is projected on the point closest to the segmentation of the support object.
At Ends: the entry point is projected on the starting point or the end point of the segmentation of the support object.

The join options are used by the entry tools and XMap Joins.
