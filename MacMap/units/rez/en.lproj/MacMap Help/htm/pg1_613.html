XMap - Constants

The Constant box displays the list of available constants.
Clicking on one of the buttons lets you run the corresponding constant.
Clicking next to one of the buttons lets you select the constant.
Click the "+" button to add a user-defined constant.
Click the "-" button to delete the selected user-defined constant.
Click the "E" button to edit the selected user-defined constant.
Click the "D" button to duplicate the selected user-defined constant.
