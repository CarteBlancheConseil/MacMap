Justify Tool
Use

The Justify tool lets you use the mouse to individually adjust the justification of the text of the objects represented by the Text View vDef. To do this, you must first specify a field for the "Justify" section. A click and drag lets you modify the value of the object field and its representation. A single click, while holding down the "Ctrl" key, causes a left justification, the "Alt" key, a center justification, and the Apple key, a right justification.
This tool also lets you adjust the letter or word spacing of these texts. To do this, you must first specify a field in the appropriate sections of the Text View vDef. You can adjust the letter or word spacing by holding down the Shift key.

Popup

This tool does not have any popups.

Options

These options let you determine if you would like to adjust the letter or word spacing.
