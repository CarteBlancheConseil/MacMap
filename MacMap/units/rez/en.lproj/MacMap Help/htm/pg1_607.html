XMap - Calcs

The Calc box displays the list of available calcs.
Clicking on one of the buttons lets you run the corresponding calc. A calc applies to a selection.
Clicking next to one of the buttons lets you select the calc.
Click the "+" button to add a user-defined calc.
Click the "-" button to delete the selected user-defined calc.
Click the "E" button to edit the selected user-defined calc.
Click the "D" button to duplicate the selected user-defined calc.
