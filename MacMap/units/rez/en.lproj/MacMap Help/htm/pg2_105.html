Length Tool
Use

This tool lets you measure the length of an object on a document. Click as many times as necessary to determine your path; the distance traveled is displayed in the mini card. To end the measurement, double-click on the document or press "Enter". The angle constraint can be accessed by holding down the "Shift" key. It is preset at 45Â°.
