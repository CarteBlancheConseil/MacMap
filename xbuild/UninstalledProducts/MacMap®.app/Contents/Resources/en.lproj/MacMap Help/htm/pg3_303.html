XMap - Topological Consistency

Topological consistency lets you preserve the geographic relationship between objects. If a line ends in a point, the movement of this point will lead to a modification of the line so that it always ends on this point. This procedure is automatic.
The "Active" checkbox lets you enable edit detection.
The "On Types" popup lets you define the types included in the topological consistency.
