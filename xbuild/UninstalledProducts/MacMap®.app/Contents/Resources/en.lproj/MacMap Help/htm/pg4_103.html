XMap - Rotate

This function lets you apply a rotation to the selected objects. This rotation must be specified in degrees/minutes, in a backward or forward direction. Two modes are available:
Around Itself: the rotation is calculated relative to the centroid of the selection.
Around a Point: the rotation is calculated relative to the indicated coordinate.

Script Command 
<rotation>
    <float>X</float>
    <float>Y</float>
    <float>Angle in degrees</float>
    <int>1(=inverse rotation)|2(=direct rotation)</int>
    <int>1(=rotation on itself)|2(=rotation on a coordinate)</int>
</rotation>
