Zoom Tool
Use

This tool lets you change the scale of the cartographic window.
A click lets you move to the next scale (from the smallest to the largest).
A click while pressing the "Shift" key lets you move to the next scale (from the largest to the smallest).
A click while pressing the "Ctrl" key shows a menu which displays the list of scales. This menu lets you center the screen on the point clicked at the selected scale.

Popup

The popup displays the list of scales. You can display the map at the desired scale.

Options

The options for this tool let you edit existing scales (value and name), delete a scale, or create a new one.
