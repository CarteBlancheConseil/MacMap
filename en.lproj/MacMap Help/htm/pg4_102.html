XMap - Dilate

This function lets you expand the selected objects into x and y coordinates. A dilation of more than 100% increases the size of the objects and a dilation of less than 100% reduces the size of the objects. Two modes are available:
Around Itself: the dilation is calculated relative to the centroid of the selection.
Around a Point: the dilation is calculated relative to the indicated coordinate.

Script Command 
<scale>
    <float>X</float>
    <float>Y</float>
    <float>Horizontal dilation percentage</float>
    <float>Vertical dilation percentage</float>
    <int>1(=dilation on itself)|2(=dilation on a coordinate)</int>
</scale>
