Translation Tool
Use

The Translation tool lets you move objects.
The objects are indicated by a selection when you move over them with the cursor. Then, a click and drag lets you relocate the object.
By holding down the "Shift" key, you force the tool to preserve the selection: when a click and drag occurs, all the selected objects are then relocated. If you hold down the "Alt" key, the relocated objects are duplicated.

Popup

This tool does not have any popups.

Options

This tool has no options.
